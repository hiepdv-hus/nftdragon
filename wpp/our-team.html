Our Team
XDRAKE Studio team consists of a board of outstanding and experienced members, who have been working in developing mobile games and Blockchain technology for a long time. The team was founded in 2016 as XGames, developed many games for cross platforms, i.e: Android, iOS, Winphone, Html5, etc.
We started focusing on developing casual games and world-trending games. Since 2018, we focused more on developing online strategy and cards games.
As Blockchain is getting more and more mature, we have also applied it to mobile platforms, especially games, with a brand new approach, pioneering in creating a new exciting, and challenging worldwide market. Everyday is a new challenge that we always try our best to conquer. As we push ourselves to our limit, we aim to create better and better games every day.
The only problem for online blockchain game is it's hard to put it on google-play or apple-store cause of the store's policy about Play To Earn feature. Because of that, we focusing to optimize the exeperience of user on browser's platform with high-end Unity 3D Developing Method. With that, user can play game directly on their browser without downloading or installing anything on their devices.
