NFTs Dragons
GlaurungSacred fire power

Fire Dragon used to be a powerful dragon race in the past. But because of its special ability, the Fire Dragon was imprisoned and being used as a weapon in the armies, gradually, they became extinct. Glaurung is a rare remaining of Fire Dragon that chosen to carry the most intense sacred flame. Glaurung's enormous body is covered with fire, it can blow fire endlessly and melt all things with its flame, even the strongest weapons.

LongweiLight of Destruction

Longwei rules the sky with the ability to absorb light as energy and use lightning as a weapon. Longwei's lightning storm allows it to attack in a large area with great damage. Possessing a majestic golden appearance, Longwei is a ledgendary dragon in the eyes of mankind.

ApophisEvil Dragon with powerful ancient powers

Apophis is an enormous creature that living and ruling the vast forests. Also from there, it can control the power of the forest, create a tough and solid cover for itself. Apophis has the ability to make a barrier, sending enemies into other territories to surprise them and then attack directly. When it comes to fighting in the jungle, Apophis is invincible.

OrochiRise of the Ice

Orochi was once an evil dragon, captured and imprisoned in the sea of ice at the North Pole. After thousands of years in prison, Orochi gradually absorbed the cold energy and became more and more powerful. Orochi broke through thousands of layers of ice that imprison him and returned with great powers. Every land Orochi passed through turned into ice and Orochi's breath brought a deadly cold that was able to kill living things in a split second.

AtorHell dragon

Legend has it that, Ator was born along with heaven and earth, a five-headed dragon monster that rules over hell and darkness. With the ability to manipulate dark powers, Ator is the nightmare of all living things. Ator slaughtered to bring them all back to hell. Ator-Dark's breath was enough to make thousands of people suffer from mental to physical pain and then slowly die in despair.
